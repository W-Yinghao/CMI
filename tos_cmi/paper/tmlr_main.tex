\paragraph{Ethics and data statement.}
Experiments use a public, de-identified EEG motor-imagery benchmark (BCI-IV-2a / BNCI2014-001) accessed via
the MOABB pipeline; no new human-subject data were collected. The study analyzes representation-level domain
(subject) information for the purpose of measuring and controlling cross-subject leakage, and reports an
honest negative/diagnostic result; we are not aware of a heightened risk of harm beyond standard EEG
benchmarking. Code to reproduce the analyses will be released as anonymous supplementary material / upon
acceptance.
