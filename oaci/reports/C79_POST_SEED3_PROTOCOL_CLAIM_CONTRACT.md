# C79P Post-Seed-3 Claim Contract

## Status

This protocol was designed after C78S outcomes and before every seed-4 learned
checkpoint or model-specific outcome.  It is an outcome-informed prospective
training-seed replication.  It is not a pre-C78S confirmation and does not repair
the historical C79 timing.

## Allowed language

- post-seed-3 prospective seed-4 replication;
- training-seed robustness under the same targets, trials, regimes, and analysis;
- seed-4-only primary decision with seed 3 as a fixed paired reference;
- exact registered F2/F4/H2 candidate qualification or nonqualification;
- registered local association and fixed-relation transport status.

## Forbidden language

- pre-C78S locked or hypothesis-generation-independent confirmation;
- independent subject, raw-data, target-population, or external validation;
- source-only, target-unlabeled, zero-label, OACI, or SRC control rescued by
  construction labels;
- nonqualification as zero effect, equivalence, or universal impossibility;
- local association as causal representation mechanism, target gauge, transport,
  actionability, selector, or deployability;
- raw or pooled p-values used to rescue a family-wise inactive result.

Raw EEG labels are already known from the shared dataset and seed-3 analysis.  The
new protected object is the seed-4 learned checkpoint/outcome field.  Seed 4 does
not introduce new subjects, targets, or trials.

All ten registered paths run unconditionally.  `active_after_Holm`, interim seed-4
branching, outcome-based checkpoint matching, and unregistered seed pooling are
forbidden.  The same-label oracle remains physically unreachable.

C79P readiness does not authorize C79E, C80, another seed, BNCI2014_004, oracle
analysis, feature/kernel/model search, or manuscript drafting.
