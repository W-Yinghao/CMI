## Scope Contract

1. T1, T3, T4, and T7 are accepted as project-internal proofs of their exact
   frozen statements under their exact frozen assumptions.

2. T2 and T6 are exact finite counterexamples. They are not general positive
   theorems and do not establish that every restricted policy reverses an
   information ordering or every positive mean effect has an adverse tail.

3. T5 remains `OPEN`. Its frozen statement may not be repaired by adding a
   decoder, disjoint-optimum condition, Fano condition, or other assumption
   inside an empirical milestone.

4. C85V independence means candidate-blind, procedurally separated internal
   rederivation followed by a separately hashed adversarial comparison and
   adjudication. It does not mean external human peer review, blinded
   mathematical refereeing, or publication acceptance.

5. T4 applies only under its frozen equal prior, common observation space,
   disjoint optimal-action sets with a valid decoder, and uniform
   nonoptimal-action regret floor `Delta`. An empirical analogy without these
   objects is not an application of T4.

6. T7 applies only under the registered pairwise sub-Gaussian MGF condition and
   a fixed valid optimal reference action. Its union bound may be loose. The
   C85T Monte Carlo examples did not establish tightness.

7. No C85 theorem, proof, counterexample, or synthetic result by itself
   establishes an empirical C84 mechanism, causal pathway, information order,
   or lower bound.

## Empirical Boundary

Any C85E bridge must distinguish exact theorem applicability from descriptive
analogy and unidentified assumptions. C84-D, C84-L4, and all C85 theorem
statuses remain immutable. C85E, C86, active acquisition, new data/model zoos,
and manuscript work are not authorized by this addendum.
