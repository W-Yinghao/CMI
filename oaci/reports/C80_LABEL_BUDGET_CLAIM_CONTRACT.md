# C80P Label-Budget Claim Contract

## Allowed readiness claim

C80P may state only that a fully specified, policy-specific existing-field
label-budget analysis is protocol-locked and technically ready for separate PI
authorization. It may report synthetic calibration and schema/provenance
replay.

## Future C80E claim boundary

A successful C80E could identify a stable design point for the exact Q0 policy,
the frozen BNCI2014-001 targets, and the existing seed-3/seed-4 candidate fields.
It would not establish a universal minimal label budget, few-label
deployability, new-subject generality, target-population generality, or external
validation.

Reliability is secondary. It is neither required for reporting actionability
nor sufficient for prediction, transport, or control. The decision frontier is
defined by held-evaluation standardized regret under physically independent
construction labels and simultaneous budget-family inference.

H2R, F2, and F4 remain exact-candidate nonqualification results. S3 is
descriptive moderation using the already registered geometry variables; it may
not become a replacement H2 model or mechanism rescue.

## Forbidden claims and actions

No C80P artifact may claim or perform real-data frontier estimation, active
label acquisition, evaluation-informed tie breaking, same-label oracle use,
target-4 primary inclusion, source-only control, target-unlabeled control,
deployability, population/dataset generality, training, forward pass,
re-inference, seed 5, BNCI2014_004, manuscript drafting, or C80E execution.
