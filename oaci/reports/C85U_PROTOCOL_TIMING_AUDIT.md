## Scientific Boundary

C85U is post-C84S exploratory infrastructure. The persisted matrix cannot
change C84-D, C84-L4, any C85 theorem status, or C85EP's historical blocker.
It creates no selector, inference result, p-value, theorem test, active policy,
C86 object, or manuscript claim.
