# B9.3 recruitment + eligibility screen — DRAFT template (development-only)

> **Gate:** real collection is gated on institutional approval BEFORE any recording; governance `status: not_started` (`b9_stage3_governance_status.md`).

> DRAFT to be adapted to the institution's approved recruitment/screening process. Not approval, not legal/IRB advice.

- **Recruitment:** institutionally-approved channels only (e.g. volunteer pool / notice boards per local policy). No coercive
  or targeted recruitment.
- **Inclusion:** healthy adult (age per local policy) able to give informed consent and complete a single EEG session.
- **Exclusion:** conditions precluding safe/valid scalp EEG (e.g. scalp lesions), inability to consent, or any
  institution-specified exclusion. Finalize to the local standard.
- **Enrollment plan:** 30 pre-declared slots (`b9_stage2_subject_slot_manifest.json`); stop after ≥24 analyzable OR all 30
  slots exhausted. "Analyzable" is fixed by predeclared quality/support (`b9_stage2_quality_criteria.json`), never by results.
- **No PII in the repository:** slot manifest + governance status record only slot indices / status codes.
