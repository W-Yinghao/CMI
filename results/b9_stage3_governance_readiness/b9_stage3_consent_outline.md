# B9.3 informed-consent OUTLINE — DRAFT template (development-only)

> **Gate:** real collection is gated on institutional approval BEFORE any recording; governance `status: not_started` (`b9_stage3_governance_status.md`).

> DRAFT content to be adapted into the institution's approved consent form. **Not** a consent form, **not** approval, **not**
> legal/IRB advice. The institution's ethics process controls the final consent language and approval.

- **Study nature:** a research EEG session for a randomized-audit *methodology* study — feasibility + statistical null
  behavior. It is **not** a medical/diagnostic test and returns **no** clinical or diagnostic result to the participant.
- **Voluntary + withdrawal:** participation is voluntary; the participant may stop or withdraw at any time without penalty;
  withdrawal handling of already-recorded data is stated per the data-management plan.
- **Procedure:** non-invasive scalp EEG; the participant views pre-assigned cues and performs the described task across
  microblocks with breaks; approximate duration and any compensation stated to the local template.
- **Risks / discomfort:** minimal (typical of non-invasive EEG — e.g. mild skin irritation from electrodes); no drugs, no
  stimulation, no clinical intervention.
- **Data + privacy:** what is recorded (EEG + event/marker logs), how it is de-identified, stored, access-controlled,
  retained/deleted, and shared — cross-referenced to `b9_stage3_data_management_plan.md`.
- **No diagnostic feedback:** the participant will not receive any interpretation of their EEG.
- **Contact + rights:** PI/ethics-board contact and participant rights per institutional policy.
