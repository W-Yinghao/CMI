\section{Related work}
\label{sec:related}

\paragraph{Probing measures encoding, not use.} Probe accuracy establishes that an attribute is
decodable, not that a model relies on it; amnesic/counterfactual probing makes the distinction
operational by intervening on the representation and observing behavior~\citep{elazar2021amnesic}. Our
L1$\leftrightarrow$L5 separation is the EEG instance of this, with head-replay $R3$ as the
representation-level intervention.

\paragraph{Concept erasure removes a signal without conferring benefit.} LEACE erases a linearly-
decodable concept in closed form with minimal collateral damage~\citep{belrose2023leace}; INLP removes
it by iterative nullspace projection~\citep{ravfogel2020inlp}; RLACE by a relaxed adversarial
game~\citep{ravfogel2022rlace}; scatter/moment-based methods target invariant subspaces via generalized
eigenproblems~\citep{ghifary2015scatter}. These are our L3 operators. We deploy them source-to-target
and show removability (L3) does not entail a target benefit (L6).

\paragraph{Domain generalization needs selection rigor.} With careful model selection and strong ERM
baselines, many invariance methods fail to beat ERM~\citep{gulrajani2021domainbed}. This is the direct
warning against reading a moving invariance proxy as a generalization gain; our closed
conditional-invariance/CMI-control premise is the EEG instance, and we deliberately propose no DG
method.

\paragraph{EEG subject identity is strong and entangled.} Audits of EEG foundation models show subject
identity is highly decodable and can confound label/cohort structure~\citep{identitytrap2026}, which is
precisely why equating ``decodable subject'' with ``harmful shortcut'' is tempting and wrong.

\paragraph{Decompose a joint effect into pathways.} A single unlabeled adaptation number conflates
geometry and prevalence/decision-prior pathways and admits an exact decomposition; FSR applies the same
measurement-then-decompose discipline to leakage, erasure, task-coupling, reliance, and consequence,
rather than collapsing them into one ``shortcut'' scalar~\citep{tos_cmi_internal,cigl_internal}.
